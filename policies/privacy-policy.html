Privacy Policy
Last updated: 15 July 2026

This policy describes how QBrew collects, uses, and protects information when you order through the app or a participating outlet brand's storefront.

Information we collect

  • Account details: name, email address, and phone number when you create an account.
  • Order information: items ordered, order type (pickup or delivery), delivery address if applicable, and order history.
  • Payment information: we never store your card details. Card payments are processed entirely by PayHere, our licensed payment gateway partner; we only receive confirmation that a payment succeeded, and the payment method used.

How we use it

To take and fulfil your orders, communicate order status, apply coupons and loyalty offers you use, and improve the menu and service at the outlets you order from. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Who we share it with

  • The specific outlet brand you're ordering from, so they can prepare and fulfil your order.
  • Supabase, our database and authentication infrastructure provider, which stores this data on our behalf under its own security and confidentiality obligations.
  • PayHere, for processing card payments.

We do not share your data across unrelated outlet brands beyond what's needed to operate the shared platform (e.g. account login).

Your choices

You can review and update your saved addresses and account details in the app at any time. To request a copy of your data or its deletion, contact privacy@qbrew.app.

Cookies and local storage

The app uses browser local storage to keep your cart and session active between visits. We do not use third-party advertising trackers.
