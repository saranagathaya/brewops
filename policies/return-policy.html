Return & Refund Policy
Last updated: 15 July 2026

QBrew operates the ordering platform used by independent coffee shop brands (each an "Outlet Brand") to take pickup and delivery orders. This policy explains how refunds work for orders placed through the app.

Freshly prepared food and drink

Because coffee and food items are made to order, we cannot accept a return of a correctly prepared item you simply changed your mind about. Refunds and remakes are offered for the situations below.

When you're entitled to a refund or remake

  • You received the wrong item, or an item was missing from your order.
  • The item had a genuine quality issue (e.g. incorrect preparation, a foreign object, spoiled ingredients).
  • Your order was cancelled by the outlet before preparation began.
  • A delivery order never arrived within a reasonable time of its estimated delivery window.

How to request one

Report the issue to the outlet directly through the app, or contact us at support@qbrew.app, within 24 hours of receiving your order. Include your order number and, where relevant, a photo of the item. We aim to resolve reports within 2 business days.

How refunds are paid

Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method used at checkout. Card payments are refunded through PayHere back to the same card; cash-on-pickup/delivery orders are refunded as store credit or by bank transfer, at your preference.
